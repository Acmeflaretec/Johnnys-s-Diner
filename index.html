About johnnys.

Welcome to Johnny's Cafe, located in the heart of Trivandrum's Kuravankinam neighborhood. At Johnny's, we take pride in offering a delectable selection of fried chicken, mouthwatering burgers, and crispy fries. Our commitment to quality ingredients and expert craftsmanship ensures every bite is packed with flavor and satisfaction. Whether you're grabbing a quick meal on the go or settling in for a leisurely dining experience, Johnny's Cafe provides a warm and welcoming atmosphere for all. Come join us and indulge in the perfect blend of taste and value at Johnny's Cafe.
